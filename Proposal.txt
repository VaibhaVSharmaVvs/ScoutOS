Scout OS (Football Scout AI) — Proposed Development Plan
🎯 Vision

Build an AI-powered football scouting platform that combines data engineering, machine learning, backend development, and LLMs to analyze any professional football player.

The system will answer questions such as:

Which position suits this player best?
Which clubs are the best tactical fit?
Which famous players are most similar?
What is their fair market value?
How will they develop over the next five years?
Why are these predictions being made?

The goal is to create a project that resembles a real scouting platform rather than a simple ML demo.
